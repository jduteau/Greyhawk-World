# Burne

"His Most Worshipful Mage of Hommlet." 8th-level magic-user living in the two-storied tower (area 31) with his partner [[Wiki/NPCs/Rufus]]. Follower of St. Cuthbert, clever, and a trifle greedy.

## Description

Young for a magic-user. Average build and dress. Often frequents the [[Wiki/Locations/Welcome-Wench-Inn]]. Careful, cautious, and not easily duped. Very conscious of his duty to protect the village and watch for evil — but all such adventures must pay him a third of the treasure.

## Role

Co-owner of the stone tower (area 31). Co-funder and builder (with Rufus) of the new castle (area 30). Member of the village council. Commander of all mage resources in the village. In exchange for Viscount's funding, they hold the area safe and report unusual happenings.

**Services available (for payment):**
- Will adventure with a party for 1/3 of total treasure
- Won't risk his life or be duped into a bad deal
- Has odd potions and scrolls in the tower (specific items per tower key)

## Party Relationship

Not yet met.

## What the Party Knows

Well-known around the village as the mage who helped defeat a large bandit force plaguing Verbobonc, and reportedly slew a green dragon in the Kron Hills (with Rufus). Openly discussed; he's a village fixture.

## GM Truth

*gm_only*

Burne is a genuine lawful good ally — but he has no loyalty to adventurers who don't pay or who act suspiciously. He and Rufus report to the Viscount of Verbobonc. A party that earns his trust (pays him, demonstrates lawful good behavior) gains access to his resources and spells. A party that seems shady will be monitored.

**Statistics:** 8th level M-U; hp 30; S 15, I 17, W 14, D 10, C 15, Ch 12. +2 ring of protection, chime of opening, magic missile wand (49 charges), +1 dagger.

**Spells memorized:**
- 1st: Burning Hands, Detect Magic, Feather Fall, Jump
- 2nd: Levitate, Mirror Image, Scare
- 3rd: Dispel Magic, Fireball, Tongues
- 4th: Fumble, Wall of Fire
- Also knows (available if needed): Comprehend Languages, Light, Read Magic, Darkness 15', Detect Invisibility, Strength, Gust of Wind, Infravision, Dimension Door, Plant Growth

## History

Came to Hommlet ~3 years ago with Rufus. Helped defeat bandit force plaguing Verbobonc. Rumored to have slain a green dragon in the Kron Hills. Was given Viscount and Archcleric funding to build the castle in exchange for holding the area and reporting.
