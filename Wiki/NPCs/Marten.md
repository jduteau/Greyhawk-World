# Marten

Former garrison soldier of [[Wiki/Locations/Keep-on-the-Borderlands]], recently mustered out. Hired by [[Wiki/PCs/Thomas]] as a man-at-arms for the Caves of Chaos expedition.

## Description

Lean, dark-haired, quick eyes. Wiry build with the coiled energy of someone who would rather be doing something than sitting still. A thin scar runs along his jawline — still pink, relatively recent. Equipment is better than a common sellsword's. Younger than [[Wiki/NPCs/Eckhardt]]; still building his reputation.

## Role

Swordsman and flanker. Hired at 1 gp/day plus a half-share of treasure. Self-described tactical role: moves, flanks, solves problems before they become bigger problems.

## Personality & Voice

- Ambitious and hungry — wants to make a name for himself
- Quick and decisive; the kind of soldier who moves on instinct rather than deliberation
- Restless energy; impatient with inaction
- Respects skill and will follow orders from someone who earns it; hot-headed but not insubordinate
- Two years on the Keep's wall before mustering out; watched too many caravans cancel
- Self-aware about his ambition without being defensive about it

## Party Relationship

[Session 2] Hired by Thomas at the Keep tavern alongside [[Wiki/NPCs/Eckhardt]]. Had been watching the party's conversation with Eckhardt before being approached. Accepted immediately and enthusiastically. Rested overnight and marched with the party back to the Caves of Chaos at first light.

## What the Party Knows

Former garrison soldier, two years at the Keep. Known to [[Wiki/NPCs/Eckhardt]] as a capable swordsman. Ambitious — explicitly stated he wants a name for himself. Hot-headed but controllable. His respect must be earned.

## History

[Session 2] Hired at the Keep tavern. Had been rotting there for weeks after mustering out, watching merchants too scared to hire and soldiers too comfortable to leave. Joined the party alongside Eckhardt. Marched with party to Caves of Chaos the following morning.
