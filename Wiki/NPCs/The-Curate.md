# The Curate

5th-level cleric and spiritual center of [[Wiki/Locations/Keep-on-the-Borderlands]]. The most influential person in the Keep aside from [[Wiki/NPCs/Sherlane]].

## Description

Rarely wears armor unless the Keep is threatened. Always carries his ring of protection +1 and snake staff. The snake staff adds +1 to hit, does 2–7 damage, and on command coils around the struck target, holding them helpless for 1d4 turns (the staff crawls back to the Curate on command). Also wields a mace +1 (D 2–7).

## Role

Leads the Chapel (area 17) with three acolytes. Empties the offering box each evening and deposits coins with the Banker. Holds the keys to the Chapel's secret compartment jointly with [[Wiki/NPCs/Sherlane]]. Will only use cure spells on members of his congregation (officers, shopkeepers — not random adventurers).

## GM Truth

*gm_only*

The Curate has a private suspicion about the spy Priest in area 7b — there is a 50% chance he will reveal this distrust if asked by someone he considers a trusted friend. His own three acolytes think very highly of the spy priest and will say so, creating an interesting contradiction the party can investigate.

**Statistics:** AC 0 (plate mail +1, normal shield, ring of protection +1), C5, hp 24, +1 to hit (high level). Mace +1 or snake staff.

**Spells:** cure light wounds, detect magic, bless, hold person.

**Hidden treasures in secret compartment under offering pedestal (area 17 — requires both the Curate's and the Castellan's keys):**
- 3 potions of healing
- 1 potion of ESP
- 1 potion of gaseous form
- Magic scroll: cure disease (higher level, cures any one normal disease), hold person (×1), cure light wounds (×3)
