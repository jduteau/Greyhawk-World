# Zert

Resident of upper room 1 at the [[Wiki/Locations/Welcome-Wench-Inn]]. 2nd level fighter, CE. Publicly poses as a traveller waiting for a caravan return from the south.

**Stats:** S 16, I 13, W 9, D 15, C 11, Ch 10 · HP 12 · Scale mail, shield, sword, dagger.

## Description

Average-looking, unremarkable. Sociable at the bar, drinks heavily — or appears to. He can consume 200% normal capacity without becoming drunk, a fact that makes him a reliable drinking companion and a useful tool for extracting information from others. Medium warhorse and lance in the stable.

## Role

Temple of Elemental Evil field agent. His cover assignment is watching for "a caravan from the south" — a plausible reason to linger indefinitely. His real work is intelligence: observing who enters the village, noting anyone heading for the Moat House, and reporting back through Temple channels.

## Party Relationship

Not yet met.

## What the Party Knows

Nothing suspicious. He is a friendly, drink-loving traveller at the inn.

## GM Truth

*gm_only*

- True role: ToEE spy, monitoring Hommlet for Temple-relevant activity
- **He does not know** that [[Wiki/NPCs/Rannos-Davi]] and Gremag at the traders (area 13) are also ToEE agents. The traders know who Zert is, but Zert does not know about them. If the party simultaneously befriends both, neither side will volunteer the connection.
- Will happily join a party for an equal share of treasure — and will just as readily betray them, help slay them when weakened, and take their goods.
- Purse in plain sight on table: small leather pouch with 40 gp, 12 ep, 27 sp. Locked coffer under clothing: 100 gems (100 gp each), 100 pp, 265 gp.
- [[Wiki/NPCs/Gundigoot]] has flagged Zert as suspicious but has not yet escalated to [[Wiki/NPCs/Jaroo-Ashstaff]].
