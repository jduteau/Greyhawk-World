# The Minotaur

A uniquely powerful minotaur inhabiting a labyrinthine cave complex (Lair I) within [[Wiki/Locations/Caves-of-Chaos]]. A mercenary who serves the [[Wiki/Factions/Bugbear-Clan-H]] in exchange for human slaves, it is arguably the most dangerous single creature in the Caves apart from the Shrine's evil priest.

## Description

Wears a great chain mail coat (unusual for a minotaur). Carries a spear +1. Its lair is decorated with skulls and bones in decorative patterns. A magical direction confusion spell pervades its labyrinth — 30' inside the entrance, adventurers lose all sense of direction and cannot map reliably (save on 19-20 each turn to throw off the effect).

## Role

Labyrinth guardian and mercenary. Hires itself out to the bugbear clan at the rate of one human slave per three days of service. Has worked for orcs and gnolls as well. Will not leave its labyrinth — the only escape from the area once inside is through the secret door to the Bugbear Chieftain's room (area 36) or by fleeing out the entrance and climbing a large tree.

## GM Truth

*gm_only*

The minotaur attacks immediately upon intruder entry — it knows its labyrinth too well for the direction confusion to disadvantage it. Combat pattern: spear first (4-9 damage due to strength), then gores and bites (1-6/1-6 per round).

The secret stone slab hiding its treasure requires 3 humans to move, and the mechanism to grasp it requires a roll of 1 on d6.

**Statistics:** AC 4 (chain mail), HD 6, hp 35, spear +1 (D 4-9 first round), then gore + bite (D 1-6/1-6), MV (40'), Save F6, ML 12.

**Treasure behind secret slab:**
- Locked chest (poison needle): 930 gp + 310 ep
- Staff of healing
- Plate mail +1 (man-sized or elf-sized)
- Locked coffer: potion of gaseous form + potion of healing + potion of growth
- Locked chest: 3 pieces of jewelry (1,600 + 900 + 600 gp)

**Alliance note:** Bugbear Chieftain will seek the minotaur's help if his lair is pressed hard enough. The minotaur-bugbear connection is the primary escalation path for Lair H.
