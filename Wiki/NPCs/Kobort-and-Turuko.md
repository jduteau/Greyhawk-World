# Kobort and Turuko

Occupants of upper room 9 at the [[Wiki/Locations/Welcome-Wench-Inn]]. A mismatched pair — a hulking fighter and a calculating monk — rooming together and watching for an opportunity.

## Kobort

2nd level fighter, neutral (not bright enough to have a genuine alignment).

**Stats:** S 18/10, I 6, W 8, D 17, C 16, Ch 11 · HP 20 · Splint mail + shield, sword, dagger. Lance, flail, axe, and a heavy warhorse in the stables.

Big, strong, and simple. He met Turuko on the road and was convinced that Turuko could make them both famous and wealthy. He does what Turuko says.

## Turuko

3rd level monk, lawful evil. A Bakluni from unknown parts.

**Stats:** S 15, I 9, W 15, D 15, C 14, Ch 5 · HP 11 · Quarterstaff + 2 daggers.

Small, thin, and watchful. Believes himself to be highly clever. Has a specific plan and is anxious to execute it, as their funds are nearly exhausted (12 gp, 5 sp, 40 cp between both of them).

## The Plan

*gm_only*

Turuko knows that there are monsters and treasure in the ruined [[Wiki/Locations/Moat-House]]. The plan: join a small adventuring party as companions, hang back during fights to avoid injury, let the party weaken itself clearing the dungeon, then slay the survivors and take everything.

- **Against a small party:** Join, accompany, betray at the right moment.
- **Against a large or obviously powerful party:** Do not join. Shadow instead, watching for a moment when deaths and wounds reduce the group to a manageable size.
- The situation is urgent for Turuko — "something must be done soon."

## Party Relationship

Not yet met. They will present as capable and enthusiastic companions looking for action.

## GM Notes

*gm_only*

- Kobort will not deviate from what Turuko tells him to do. He is not cruel by nature; he simply follows the plan without moral reflection.
- Turuko's "lawful" component means he will honor any explicit commitment he makes — but he will avoid making them. He operates by omission and timing, not by breaking agreements.
- If the party is clearly stronger than expected, Turuko may abandon the plan and become a genuine if mercenary companion. His self-preservation instinct is strong.
- The monk's key combat ability at 3rd level: unarmed strikes in addition to weapons; limited AC reduction from Wisdom; open hand attacks. The quarterstaff + daggers are primary weapons.
