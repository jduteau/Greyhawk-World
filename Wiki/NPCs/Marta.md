# Marta

Farmer's wife. Around thirty years old. Present at the Keep tavern (area 15) during the party's first visit.

## Description

Quietly composed in the way of someone frightened long enough that the fear has become ordinary. She cannot pay. She does not beg — she states facts and lets them speak.

Her husband [[Wiki/NPCs/Edric]] left three weeks ago to sell grain at the market. He never arrived.

## Role

The missing-person thread. Edric is a prisoner in the [[Wiki/Locations/Caves-of-Chaos]]. His fate follows from which caves the party explores and how quickly they move.

## Party Relationship

Not yet met.

## What the Party Knows

Nothing yet. She is present at the Keep tavern during the party's first visit.

## GM Notes

*gm_only*

Her manner is the point — she is not overwrought, she is past that. She is doing the only thing left she can: asking plainly. The party either responds to this or doesn't.

Edric's survival is time-sensitive. The party's pace through the Caves determines whether they find a live prisoner or a dead one. Place Edric in a lair the party is unlikely to reach on their first expedition.
