# Nira Melubb

Proprietor of the moneychanger's shop (area 15) in [[Wiki/Locations/Village-of-Hommlet]]. 3rd level thief (non-active). The sign shows three yellow balls.

**Stats:** S 9, I 16, W 13, D 15, C 9, Ch 10 · HP 12 · +1 dagger (boot), +1 sword (under counter).

## Description

Always obliging, polite, and businesslike. Asks no questions. Has a guard near the door (3rd level fighter, HP 17, chain mail, crossbow and sword) and two war dogs (2+2 HD, HP 16/13, 2-8 hp bite each) always nearby.

## Services

- **Coin exchange:** Any coins or metal for other exchange media; 10% fee on value.
- **Gems and jewelry:** Buys at 50–80% of actual value; sells at 102–120% above actual; fabricates on commission (cost = materials value × 2, which becomes the new item's value).
- **Restocking:** Can obtain new gem/jewelry stock every 7–12 days from passing merchants at market value. Track transactions — once supply is exhausted, it stays exhausted until resupply.

## What the Party Knows

Neutral, discreet moneychanger. Fair but not cheap. Good service, no questions. Well-defended.
