# Y'dey

*Stub — full detail in T2 (The Temple of Elemental Evil).*

Canoness of the Church of St. Cuthbert in [[Wiki/Locations/Village-of-Hommlet]]. 6th level cleric. Currently away on a quest; [[Wiki/NPCs/Terjon]] is her temporary replacement and is not pleased about it.

## Current Status

Absent from Hommlet. Traveling with:
- **Otis** — 10th level ranger; Elmo's elder brother (see [[Wiki/NPCs/Elmo]])
- **Murfles** — elf fighter/thief 1st/2nd level

The nature and destination of the quest are not detailed in T1 — see T2 (The Temple of Elemental Evil) for full information.

## Hidden Cache

*gm_only*

Y'dey left a personal cache concealed in the fireplace mantle of the **Chief Priest's Chamber** (area 14) in the Church of St. Cuthbert. **Only Y'dey knows this is here.** [[Wiki/NPCs/Terjon]] has not found it and does not know it exists.

Contents:
- Bronze neck chain set with 6 × 1,000 gp rubies + amber cudgel pendant (total: **10,000 gp**)
- +1 mace
- Scroll of protection from demons

## GM Notes

*gm_only*

- Terjon's unhappiness at being left in charge is a character hook: if the party helps advance St. Cuthbert's cause significantly, Terjon may warm to them and become a useful ally — and may eventually mention Y'dey's expected return.
- If players find the cache: it belongs to Y'dey, not the church. Taking it creates an obligation to her when she returns.
- Otis (Elmo's brother): if anything happens to [[Wiki/NPCs/Elmo]], "Otis will eventually hear of it and seek redress." Otis at 10th level ranger is a serious threat to anyone who harms his brother.
