# Chief Ishka

Chief of [[Wiki/Factions/Kobold-Tribe-A]] in Lair A of [[Wiki/Locations/Caves-of-Chaos]]. Named during the party's first parley with the kobolds in Session 2.

## Description

Larger than other kobolds. Wears a crude iron crown sitting askew on his head and a headdress of rat skulls and feathers. Seated on a throne of stacked stones and animal skulls when the party met him. Cold, unblinking reptilian eyes. Bitter humor — laughs without mirth when lied to.

The kobold shaman ([[Wiki/NPCs/Kobold-Shaman]]) speaks Common and acts as his translator and, in this session, as a voice of pragmatic counsel that eventually overrode Ishka's preference for silence.

## Role

Absolute leader of Kobold Tribe A. Responsible for the tribe's survival in an increasingly hostile cave environment. His tribe has been squeezed from above by goblins and from across the ravine by orcs. What previously looked like normal tribal conflict has shifted — the other tribes have stopped fighting each other and begun working in concert, which threatens the kobolds' already marginal position.

## Personality & Voice

- Hostile and suspicious by default — expects big-ones to lie, then kill
- Not irrational; willing to make a deal if the terms are sufficiently favorable to the tribe's survival
- Bitter about the tribe's diminished position; aware they are too weak to do anything about the deep threat themselves
- Allowed the shaman to speak over his own objections, which suggests he understood the trade-off even if he didn't like it
- Holds the party to their word; "kobolds have long memories"

## Party Relationship

[Session 2] First contact. Initially demanded the party leave immediately. Shaman overrode this to reveal intelligence after Thomas appealed directly to the shaman's fear. Ishka grudgingly acquiesced, then made an implicit deal: party deals with the deep threat; the Keep leaves the kobolds alone. Relationship is transactional and fragile — trust must be earned through results.

## What the Party Knows

Chief of the kobold tribe. Wore an iron crown. Hostile but not mindlessly so. Made a deal: party clears the deep threat, kobolds get left alone by the Keep. Has long memory; will hold the party to their word.

## GM Truth

*gm_only*

Ishka's module stats: AC 5, HD 2, hp 8, battle axe (D 2-8). Carries key to Room 3 (food storage). Has a large gem on golden chain (1,200 gp). Hidden 50 gp sewn into a wall-hanging blanket. Locked chest: 203 cp + 61 sp + 22 ep.

The name "Ishka" is emergent — not given in B2. His stats are canon.

The deal with the party creates a potential complication: if the party later raids the kobold lair, Ishka will fight to the death and the tribe will regard the party as oath-breakers. If the party clears Lair K and reports back to the Keep, Ishka expects a formal arrangement guaranteeing the tribe's safety — something Sherlane would likely accept as practical rather than principled.

## History

[Session 2] First meeting with the party. Granted audience under protest after the shaman's intervention. Made implicit deal regarding the deep caves threat. Allowed the party to depart without harm after intelligence was shared.
