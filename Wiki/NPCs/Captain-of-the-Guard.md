# Captain of the Guard

Commander of the Inner Gatehouse garrison at [[Wiki/Locations/Keep-on-the-Borderlands]]. A 3rd-level fighter of uncommon skill and good character, notable for his friendly manner and occasional undercover patrols in the Outer Bailey.

## Description

No physical description given in the text. Equipped with plate mail +1, shield +1, sword +2, and spear +1. Carries a dagger whose pommel contains a 150 gp gem. Has 15 gp on his person.

## Role

Commands all guards stationed in the Inner Gatehouse (area 18) and is responsible for security of the Inner Bailey. Sometimes moves through the Outer Bailey in disguise as a mercenary fighter — an unofficial intelligence habit.

## GM Truth

*gm_only*

The Captain is one of the most capable fighters in the Keep after the Castellan. He is genuinely kind and friendly — not just professionally courteous. If the party treats mercenaries in the tavern (area 15) with respect or generosity, there is a chance one of those mercenaries is actually him in disguise; a strong early impression could yield a useful internal ally before the party ever meets him officially.

**Statistics:** AC 0 (plate mail +1, shield +1), F3, hp 24, +1 to hit (magic bonus), sword +2, spear +1, dagger (150 gp gem in pommel), ML 11.

**On his person:** 15 gp, dagger with 150 gp gem in pommel.
