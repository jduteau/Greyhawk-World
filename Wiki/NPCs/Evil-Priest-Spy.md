# Evil Priest (Spy)

A chaotic evil cleric disguised as a jovial, theologically curious visitor staying in the Keep's private apartments (area 7b). A field agent for [[Wiki/Factions/Temple-of-Evil-Chaos]] who infiltrates adventuring parties and betrays them inside the [[Wiki/Locations/Caves-of-Chaos]].

## Description

Robust appearance (18 hp). Always wears his ring and carries his mace +1. When armored: plate mail +1, shield +1, AC −1 (Dex 15). His two acolytes never speak — he claims they follow vows of silence until they attain priestly standing (cover story). Everyone in the Keep speaks well of him. Guests are welcomed with a cozy fire and plenty of ale or wine.

## Role

Ostensibly: a visiting cleric discussing theology and converting travelers, stopping over between caravan routes. Actually: monitoring expeditions to the Caves of Chaos and sabotaging successful adventuring parties before they threaten the Shrine.

## Party Relationship

Not yet met. He will be enthusiastically friendly if the party visits area 7b.

## What the Party Knows

Nothing yet.

## GM Truth

*gm_only*

**The Betrayal:** If approached by a party seeking the Caves of Chaos, he will volunteer to join them. Once inside the Caves during a critical encounter, he will use cause light wounds (2–7 damage, touch attack) or light (cast on an opponent's eyes to blind them) to hinder or harm party members. Betrayal always occurs at the worst possible moment for the adventurers.

**Combat resources:**
- Plate mail +1, shield +1 (AC −1 with Dex 15), mace +1
- Cleric scroll: hold person + silence 15' radius
- Wears gold chain (100 gp) + bloodstone gem (500 gp extra)
- Two acolytes: AC 4, C1, hp 7 each; plate mail + shield + mace; each wears gold chain (100 gp)

**Hidden bribe/escape fund (sack concealed in his chair):**
30 each of pp, gp, ep, sp, cp + jeweled clasp (300 gp)

**Intelligence network:** The spy monitors the entry scribe's records in area 3 to track which adventurers enter and leave the Keep and when. This information is relayed to the Shrine.

**Discovery clue:** [[Wiki/NPCs/The-Curate]] privately distrusts this priest — 50% chance to say so if asked by a trusted friend. The Curate's own acolytes contradict him and speak well of the spy, which the party can notice as suspicious once the Curate's distrust is known.
