# Eckhardt

Former garrison soldier of [[Wiki/Locations/Keep-on-the-Borderlands]], recently mustered out. Hired by [[Wiki/PCs/Thomas]] as a man-at-arms for the Caves of Chaos expedition.

## Description

Broad-shouldered, middle-aged. Plain, unremarkable face. Cropped hair. Moves with the patient stillness of a man who has learned to wait. His chain mail shows signs of wear but is carefully oiled. His spear is well-maintained. Watches rooms without appearing to watch them.

Dull for conversation, but that was never the point. He carries himself with the economy of a career soldier — no wasted motion, no unnecessary speech.

## Role

Spearman and line-holder. Hired at 1 gp/day plus a half-share of treasure. Terms negotiated directly with Thomas: one voice gives orders, no heroics, no running, no panicking. Requests only that if he falls, his body be brought back for proper burial if it can be managed.

## Personality & Voice

- Steady as stone; does not break or panic
- Follows orders without question, but will refuse anything he judges as pure foolishness
- Professional: asked about single command structure before accepting the job; evaluates employers before committing
- Economy of speech; says what he means; offers observations without advocacy
- Two years at the Keep before mustering out; went looking for caravan work that wasn't coming

## Party Relationship

[Session 2] Hired by Thomas at the Keep tavern on recommendation from the corporal of the watch. Joined the party immediately. Rested overnight and marched with the party back to the Caves of Chaos at first light. Has not yet been tested in combat with this group.

## What the Party Knows

Former garrison soldier, two tours at the Keep. Known to the corporal of the watch as reliable. Waiting for caravan work that dried up. Rates himself as steady but doesn't boast. Single command structure is a firm condition.

## History

[Session 2] Hired at the Keep tavern. Mustered out of the Keep garrison approximately three weeks prior. Was waiting for caravan work. Joined the party after Thomas presented a convincing case and Eckhardt confirmed the single-command structure he requires. Marched with party to Caves of Chaos the following morning.
