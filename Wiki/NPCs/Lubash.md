# Lubash

Named ogre guarding room 7 of the [[Wiki/Locations/Moat-House]] dungeon level. Recruited into Temple service by [[Wiki/NPCs/Lareth-the-Beautiful]] with the promise of food (including human flesh) and treasure.

**Stats:** AC 5 · HD 5+1 · HP 21 · Move 9" · Bardiche (2-8 +5 = 7–13 damage).

## Description

A large, hungry ogre with a nest of old clothing and skins in the corner of his room. His treasure hoard is mostly worthless: glass beads, brass candlesticks, and trash — plus 823 cp, 46 sp, 3 gp. By some twist of fate, a small-sized elven cloak is intermixed in the carpet and rags of his nest.

## Role

Guardian of the central dungeon passage. He has been instructed to allow only those wearing the **yellow eye of fire** symbol OR who know the proper **secret sign** (crossed arms before the face). He will not attack immediately — he waits to see if the challenge is answered. If it is not, he attacks.

He will devour anyone he kills at the first opportunity. He tosses wounded and prisoners into the adjoining room (area 8, his pantry) for later consumption. He will pursue fleeing parties, but no further than the stairs up (area 4, above).

## GM Notes

*gm_only*

- Area 8 (his pantry) currently contains two merchants (mildly tortured, will promise 100 sp each as reward, paid via caravan to Hommlet four weeks after release) and a gnome caught spying by gnolls (will give a plain iron ring — recognized 70% of the time by gnomes within 100 miles as a sign of alliance).
- The elven cloak in his nest requires active searching; it is not immediately visible.
- Lubash is aware that "the Master" is somewhere to the south, but does not know details.
