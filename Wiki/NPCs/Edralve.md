# Edralve

Drow intermediary operating within the [[Wiki/Factions/Brotherhood-of-Slave-Lords]] network. The slavers' senior leadership receives direction from her and believes she is a well-connected fence. She is not.

## Description

Appeared twice yearly to slaver contacts. Always hooded. Smelled of deep stone. Never ate in company. "Not human, I think" — a captured Slavelord's description under interrogation. Paid in magic items rather than coin.

## Role

The drow insertion point into the slaver network. She launders drow direction as commercial patronage — the slavers believe they have a silent backer with unusual resources and connections. They do not know she is drow, and they do not know what she ultimately serves.

Her presence explains why the Brotherhood has access to drow-crafted items (manacles unlike anything surface smiths make) and why their operations route toward "the lower road" — a path into the Underdark.

## Party Relationship

Not yet encountered. Unknown to exist.

## What the Party Knows

Nothing. The breadcrumbs (A1-4 phase) point toward an unnamed hooded figure before the party can learn her name.

## GM Truth

*gm_only*

Edralve is a trace character — the party learns she exists from evidence and testimony before (if ever) they encounter her directly. Her physical encounter, if it occurs, is in the A1-4 arc.

Her Lolth connection is the link between the surface slaver network and the deeper conspiracy. She is one level above Lareth in the chain — she receives direction from Eclavdra or through similar drow channels, not from Lareth himself.
