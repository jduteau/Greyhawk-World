# Elmo

A 4th-level ranger based in [[Wiki/Locations/Village-of-Hommlet]], presenting as a simple, slow-talking, hard-drinking farmhand. Actually an undercover agent of the Viscount of Verbobonc, talent-spotting adventurers at the [[Wiki/Locations/Welcome-Wench-Inn]].

## Description

Massive strength (S 18/43). Slow and halting speech. Often appears tipsy and jovial — he is not. Carries a +2 dagger at all times ("My brudder Otis gave it to me!" — volunteers this if asked about the dagger). Lives at home on the family farm (area 2) but spends significant time at the Welcome Wench.

## Role

Frequents the inn to observe all newcomers. Will insinuate himself into expeditions heading to the Moat House. Ostensibly motivated by payment and ale money; genuinely motivated by the Viscount's intelligence mission.

**How to hire him:** He wants MONEY (for ale) and asks that the employer furnish him armor and a "BIG axe." If hired on these terms, he will secretly wear his own +1 chain mail in addition to (or in place of) whatever is purchased, and will use his hidden magic axe.

Nothing yet. He appears to be a strong but dim, beer-loving farmhand.

## GM Truth

*gm_only*

Elmo is a plant. He reports to the Viscount of Verbobonc. His intelligence mission is to observe activity in Hommlet, identify adventuring parties, and embed himself with promising groups heading to the Moat House. He is smarter than he seems, completely sober regardless of how much he drinks, and a capable combatant.

**Statistics:** S 18/43, I 15, W 16, D 16, C 17, Ch 4; 5 HD, 41 hp; +1 chain mail, +2 shield, +1 battleaxe (all hidden), +2 dagger (carried always).

**Hidden gear (lead-lined oaken chest buried in barn floor, area 2):**
- +1 chain mail
- +2 shield
- +1 battleaxe
- 6 gems (100 gp each)
- 10 pp, 50 gp, 100 sp

**His brother Otis** is a 10th-level ranger, currently away on a quest with Y'dey (the absent Canoness) and Murfles (an elf fighter/thief). If anything bad happens to Elmo, Otis will eventually hear of it and seek redress. Otis's return with Y'dey is relevant to the T2 arc.

**Faction clarification:** Elmo opposes the Temple of Elemental Evil — his faction field references the enemy faction for tracking purposes. His true allegiance is to the Viscounty of Verbobonc and lawful good.

## Debrief Hooks

*gm_only*

If the party reaches the Moat House dungeon level, they have gathered exactly the intelligence Elmo's mission requires. Whether he was embedded with them or learns of it afterward:

- **First confirmation:** This is the Viscount's first confirmation that the Moat House reoccupation is commanded rather than opportunistic. Elmo escalates immediately through his normal channels.
- **Lareth's identity:** If told Lareth's name, clerical allegiance (Lolth), or level — escalate further. A 5th-level CE Lolth cleric with 44 HP and a staff of striking is a Viscount-level threat, not a Moat House problem.
- **The treasure as a tell:** If the party describes the fire opals, silver service, or the overall wealth of Lareth's quarters, Elmo recognizes this as Temple-grade resourcing. Ordinary bandits do not keep 45,000 gp necklaces in a dungeon room.
- **Reward vector:** Elmo has no personal wealth to offer, but he can open the door to direct Viscount patronage — official recognition, castle resources, and the intelligence structure behind Burne and Rufus. This is the hook that brings T1 into the broader Greyhawk political arc.
