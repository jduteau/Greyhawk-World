# Edric

Farmer. Husband of [[Wiki/NPCs/Marta]]. Left three weeks before campaign start to sell grain at the market and never arrived. One of several travellers who have gone missing on the Hommlet–Keep road — taken, not killed.

## Description

No physical description established yet.

## Role

A live stake in the [[Wiki/Locations/Caves-of-Chaos]]. His capture was deliberate — part of the coordinated pattern of abductions the humanoid tribes have been conducting along the road. Drag marks on the road near the site of his disappearance confirm he was taken alive.

## GM Truth

*gm_only*

Edric is a prisoner in the Caves. Exact placement is GM's call — recommended: a lair the party does not reach on their first expedition, to keep the time pressure meaningful.

His survival is consequential. If the party moves quickly enough to reach him, he is alive and grateful. If they delay or skip his lair, he is dead when they arrive — or never found. Either outcome is valid.

He is not a combatant. He cannot fight his way out. He needs the party to come to him.
