# Gremag

Partner and co-proprietor of the trading establishment (area 13) in [[Wiki/Locations/Village-of-Hommlet]], alongside [[Wiki/NPCs/Rannos-Davi]].

**Stats:** S 15, I 12, W 7, D 16, C 16, Ch 14 · HP 39 · +2 chain shirt, dagger of venom, ice dust (3 uses in pouch).

## Description

Tall, thin, with sharp features and protruding eyes. Tends to dither and fuss — at Rannos Davi and at customers alike. The manner reads as anxious and slightly incompetent; this is dangerous misdirection. He is an assassin.

His public role is merchant clerk: he handles stock, haggles with buyers, fusses over prices and inventory. His private role is enforcement and elimination.

## Role

The "even more chaotic" half of the trading pair. Where Rannos Davi is cautious and suspicious, Gremag is volatile. Both are Temple servants, both report adventuring activity via brigand courier, both render aid to Temple agents passing through.

Gremag is the more immediately dangerous of the two. He is armed for assassination, not commerce.

## Personality & Voice

- Fussy and apologetic in manner — constantly straightening things, qualifying his prices, glancing at Rannos Davi for confirmation on larger decisions; reads as mildly incompetent
- Talks too much when nervous, which is increasingly often — the verbal noise is partly professional habit, partly genuine anxiety leaking through
- Protruding-eyed stare that makes people slightly uncomfortable; he overcompensates with excessive helpfulness
- The assassin underneath is absolutely silent; if Gremag ever goes cold and stops talking, something bad is about to happen

## Goals

- Maintain cover as merchant partner; process Temple intelligence flowing through the trading post without drawing suspicion
- Manage his growing unease with the scale of Lareth's operation — he wants a quiet exit strategy that doesn't cost him his life
- Execute elimination tasks if the party becomes a serious threat to Temple operations (this is his actual value to the Temple)
- Collect his pay and stay alive; the ideology was never the point

## Knowledge

- Knows the TZGY scarab, the dead drop system, and Lareth's code name "the Shepherd" — but has never met Lareth in person
- Knows Zert is a Temple agent at the Welcome Wench; Zert does not know about him — this asymmetry is a vulnerability if discovered
- Knows the general pattern of raiding party movements (they travel in traders' wagons) and what territory is off-limits
- Knows his ice dust and dagger of venom are not defensive tools — if directly confronted, he is more likely to run or crack than fight

## GM Truth

*gm_only*

- 7th level assassin with fully loaded kit. The dagger of venom and ice dust are not defensive weapons.
- He and Rannos know about [[Wiki/NPCs/Zert]] (the ToEE spy at the Welcome Wench inn). Zert does not know about them.
- **Joined for money, not ideology.** Gremag is increasingly uncomfortable with how large the operation has become. He did not sign on to run a supply chain for a Caves raiding network — he signed on for a quiet intelligence role with good pay. The scale of Lareth's operation is not what he expected.
- **Pressure point:** Gremag is the more likely of the two to break under sustained pressure. He is nervous, and that nervousness is readable if the party pays attention. Rannos Davi is cold and calculating; Gremag flinches.
- Like Rannos, he knows Lareth only as "the Shepherd" — has never met him in person; communication through dead drops on the Hommlet–Moat House road.
- The TZGY scarab (Temple pass) is in the locked coffer in their back room, alongside 150 pp, 300 gp, 50 × 100 gp gems, and 2 potions of invisibility.
- The traders' groom (rear barn) is secretly a CE bandit assigned to track party activity and report to the traders.
- If the party becomes sufficiently threatening to Temple interests, Gremag is the one likely to act directly. Rannos Davi coordinates; Gremag executes.
