# Eclavdra

Drow noblewoman of House Eilservs. The architect of the giant destabilization campaign (G1-3) and a field operative for Lolth's broader conspiracy. First physical drow appearance in the campaign — do not introduce her before G3.

## Description

Aristocratic, coldly beautiful. Utterly unconcerned in the presence of danger until she realizes the party is genuinely capable of ending the operation. At that point she leaves with contemptuous efficiency.

Her presence in G3 (Hall of the Fire Giant King) is the moment the conspiracy becomes visible. Before this, she exists only as indirect traces — the giants' "dark lady," the enchanted weapons, the distant patron.

## Role

- Supplied giant chiefs (G1-3) with enchanted weapons and promises of surface territory
- Physically present in G3; escapes when the party arrives, collapsing the entrance behind her
- Represents House Eilservs — a minority faction within drow society; her operation was not sanctioned by all drow houses

## Party Relationship

Not yet encountered. Unknown to exist.

## What the Party Knows

Nothing. "The dark lady" — a giant's phrase for their patron — is the only breadcrumb before G3.

## GM Truth

*gm_only*

Eclavdra escapes at G3. A hobgoblin mercenary guard saw her go through a hidden door in the lowest level — he will tell the party where, for the right price. This is the trail into D1-3.

Her operation at the surface was not officially sanctioned by Lolth or by the full drow council. Some houses are horrified that it has attracted surface-world attention. Lolth herself has said nothing — which means she is pleased.

Eclavdra is not destroyed at G3. She retreats to the Underdark. The party follows.
