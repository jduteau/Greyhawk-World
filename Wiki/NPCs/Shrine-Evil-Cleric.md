# Shrine Evil Cleric

The 3rd-level chaotic evil cleric who commands the [[Wiki/Factions/Temple-of-Evil-Chaos]] shrine complex in Lair K of the [[Wiki/Locations/Caves-of-Chaos]].

## Description

Wears black cape and cowl with red robes beneath. Plate mail +1 and shield +1 beneath robes. Armed with a snake staff (+1 to hit, D 2–7; on command coils around the struck target, holding them helpless for 1d4 turns — the staff crawls back on command) and a normal mace at his belt. Wears a gold ring with a black gem (1,400 gp) and carries 51 pp.

## Role

Absolute master of the Shrine. Commands zombie guards, acolytes, adepts, and the hall of undead warriors. Conducts evil rites at the three-altar temple (room K-58). Has captured a medusa (room K-64) and plans to sacrifice her at a special rite after removing her snakes and blinding her.

## GM Truth

*gm_only*

**Statistics:** AC 0 (plate+1, shield+1), or −1 vs. good creatures (amulet of protection from good adds +1 save, −1 opponent to-hit vs. the wearer), C3, hp 14.

**Spells:** cause light wounds (2–7 damage, requires touch-to-hit roll), cause fear (failed save = flee in terror 1 turn, requires touch-to-hit).

**Scroll:** detect magic, hold person, silence 15' radius.

**Escape:** Potion of gaseous form — will use to escape via boulder-filled corridor (K-51) as a last resort. Escape route: slip behind southeast screen → enter wardrobe → secret door in back → short passage → corridor. When the wardrobe's secret door is opened by pursuers, 500 gp + 50 gems (10 gp each) spill into the room — a literal loot trap to slow pursuit.

**Forces available (total):**
- 3 plate-mailed zombies in anteroom K-59g (AC 2, hp 13 each, turned as ghouls via amulets)
- 8 zombies in guard room K-53 (turned as ghouls via amulets)
- 8 wandering zombies (summoned by noise throughout Shrine)
- 4 acolytes (C1) in room K-54 — red robes, chain mail underneath
- 4 adepts (C2) in room K-56 — black robes, plate mail underneath
- 20 skeletons + 20 zombies in hall K-57 — deploy to temple walls when the great bell sounds
- 12 skeletons in Hall of Skeletons K-52 — dormant until dais is touched

**Coordination with spy:** [[Wiki/NPCs/Evil-Priest-Spy]] in the Keep (area 7b) serves as the cleric's field agent, feeding intelligence on adventuring parties.

## Lolth Conspiracy Context

*gm_only*

The Shrine is Layer 2 of [[Wiki/Conspiracy/Lolth-Overarching-Conspiracy]]. Cultists know only their immediate handlers. The Shrine Evil Cleric himself does not know the full chain above him.

**Spider amulet breadcrumb (B2 phase):** The Shrine Evil Cleric wears a silver amulet of unusual workmanship — a spider worked in dark metal, with tiny inscriptions around the rim. The amulet is cold to the touch regardless of ambient temperature, slightly heavier than it should be. It was a gift; he doesn't know from whom. He received it through a coastal merchant contact.

A character with Read Magic can decipher the inscription — a name or title in the same angular script as the drow coin in Lareth's strongbox. These two clues, found in different locations, share a script. That recognition is the first thread.
