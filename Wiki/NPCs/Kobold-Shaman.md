# Kobold Shaman

Unnamed shaman and advisor to [[Wiki/NPCs/Chief-Ishka]] in [[Wiki/Factions/Kobold-Tribe-A]], Lair A of [[Wiki/Locations/Caves-of-Chaos]]. The tribe's only Common-speaker; the primary source of intelligence in Session 2.

## Description

Thinner than other kobolds. Clutches a gnarled staff. Wears a crude necklace of bones and teeth. Speaks broken Common with a thick hissing accent — translates for Chief Ishka, but in Session 2 spoke for himself when the stakes exceeded protocol. Visibly frightened of something deeper in the caves, not of the party.

## Role

Shaman and Common-speaker for Kobold Tribe A. Acts as translator between Chief Ishka and outside parties. In practice, provides a pragmatic second voice that is willing to consider deals Ishka would refuse — because the shaman has been listening to the chanting and knows the kobolds cannot survive what is coming without outside help.

## Intelligence Provided

The most significant intelligence source the party has found in Session 2:

- The cave tribes (goblins, orcs, gnolls) are being commanded by an unknown force, working together despite ancient enmities
- Prisoners are being taken alive into the deep caves — not for food, not for ransom, for unknown purposes
- The kobolds hear chanting at night emanating from the deep
- Something old dwells in the large cave at the ravine's northern end
- Even the rats have fled from the deep caves
- The kobolds feel a "wrongness" from that direction

## Personality & Voice

- More frightened of the deep evil than of the adventurers — this was the key lever Thomas used
- Pragmatic where Ishka is stubborn; willing to trade intelligence for survival guarantees
- Speaks in broken, urgent bursts when pushed past protocol
- Holds the tribe's interests above Ishka's face-saving impulses when survival is on the line

## Party Relationship

[Session 2] Initially translating for Ishka (hostile, deflecting). Broke with Ishka's orders when Thomas appealed directly to his fear. Argued with Ishka until the chief grudgingly allowed him to speak. Provided full intelligence on the deep cave situation. Warned: "If the thing in the deep learns kobolds talked to you, we are dead anyway."

## What the Party Knows

The shaman speaks Common, unlike the chief. Broke ranks with Ishka to share intelligence. Frightened of the deep. The information he provided about coordinated tribes, living prisoners, and chanting at night is the party's primary evidence for reporting to Sherlane.

## GM Truth

*gm_only*

Unnamed in the module — this is an emergent character. The shaman's willingness to deal gives the party a potential ongoing intelligence contact if they return and uphold their promise to the kobolds. If the party raids the lair, the shaman will be among the combatants — no special protection.

His fear of the deep is accurate. The Shrine of Evil Chaos (Lair K) is the source of the chanting and the wrongness. The "coordinated command" the shaman describes is [[Wiki/NPCs/Shrine-Evil-Cleric]]'s influence, not a single mastermind — the Shrine's clerics are the organizing force, not a single named general.

## History

Broke from his role as translator to argue with Ishka for the right to speak, then delivered the key intelligence about the deep caves. Warned the party of the consequence of the kobolds being discovered talking.
