# Spugnoir

Occupant of upper room 5 at the [[Wiki/Locations/Welcome-Wench-Inn]]. 2nd level magic-user, CN. Arrived with a merchant wagon and is quietly waiting for an opportunity to search the [[Wiki/Locations/Moat-House]] for spell scrolls.

**Stats:** S 11, I 15, W 11, D 15, C 14, Ch 7 · HP 4 · Dagger only. Wears non-descript clothes to avoid being recognized as a magic-user.

**Known spells:** detect magic, read magic, sleep (only).

## Description

Nondescript and forgettable by design. Carries himself like a merchant's clerk. A large trunk in his room holds his magical paraphernalia, clothing, and a fair assortment of dungeon exploration gear — the equipment belies the plain appearance. He learned that "a warlock was housed" at the ruined Moat House and has come hoping to find spell scrolls to expand his very limited repertoire.

## Role

Opportunistic dungeon prospector. No political agenda, no faction affiliation. Wants scrolls.

## Party Relationship

Not yet met. If approached and offered **all scrolls found**, he will join the party. Otherwise he will attempt to shadow the group and claim what he can from a distance. He is not a combat asset (4 hp, dagger) and he knows it.

## What the Party Knows

Not yet revealed.

## GM Notes

*gm_only*

- Spugnoir's presence is a signpost: a wandering M-U with dungeon gear who arrived recently and is asking about the Moat House tells a savvy party that something interesting is out there.
- [[Wiki/NPCs/Gundigoot]] is aware that upper room 5 has a "bookish fellow asking about the old moat house." He mentions this conversationally to parties asking about the location.
- If shadowing the party rather than joining: Spugnoir will benefit from their dungeon-clearing but will not report back or share information. If he finds scrolls independently, he will not mention them.
- Funds: 11 pp, 9 gp, 7 sp in wallet; 3 × 50 gp gems sewn into the hem of his cloak.
