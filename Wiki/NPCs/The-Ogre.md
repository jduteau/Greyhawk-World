# The Ogre

A wealthy mercenary ogre inhabiting Lair E in [[Wiki/Locations/Caves-of-Chaos]]. Has grown rich serving as a hired blade — primarily for the [[Wiki/Factions/Goblin-Tribe-D]], but available to any faction that can outbid them. Bribable by the party as well. Uses a huge bear hide as a bed.

## Description

Enormous. Wears a thick bearskin as additional protection on top of his naturally tough hide (AC 4 total). The western part of his cave has what appears to be a bear sleeping — it is the bearskin bed. The eastern part is where he actually sits. His lair smells strongly of sour odor.

## Role

Freelance muscle. The goblins keep a sack of 250 gp in a water barrel in Room 18 specifically to pay him in an emergency — tossed through a secret door to his lair with a request for help. He responds immediately for that fee. Has also been hired by the orcs and gnolls.

## GM Truth

*gm_only*

The Ogre is the most straightforwardly negotiable combatant in the Caves — he responds to gold, not ideology.

**Bribe mechanics:**
- If offered more than the goblins' standing 250 gp payment, he will take BOTH payments 90% of the time, then return to his lair without helping either side.
- Offer of 20+ gp value: 90% chance he lets the party pass unmolested. However, if he encounters the party again, he will attack regardless of prior agreement.

**Statistics:** AC 4 (thick hide + bearskin), HD 4+1, hp 25, D 3-12 (1d10+2, Strength), MV (30'), Save F4, ML 10.

**Treasure (Room 22, 7 sacks in a great leather bag):**
- #1: 287 sp
- #2: hard cheese (food)
- #3: 182 cp + 91 ep
- #4: 289 gp
- #5: keg of brandy (80 gp)
- #6: 303 cp
- #7: 241 "gold" coins — actually lead with a gold wash, worth 1 cp each

**Hidden under a heap of bones (south cave — requires searching):**
- 6 magic arrows +1
- Potion of invisibility
- Scroll with 2 cleric spells: cure light wounds + hold person

The bone heap treasure is the most valuable find in Lair E and is easily missed.
