# The Advisor

A 3rd-level elf serving as senior counselor to [[Wiki/NPCs/Sherlane]] at [[Wiki/Locations/Keep-on-the-Borderlands]]. Quartered in the Central Towers (area 26). His high Dexterity and magic-enhanced armor make him the second most defensively capable person in the Keep after the Castellan.

## Description

No physical description given. Wears plate mail +1 and a ring of fire resistance. Carries a short bow used at +2 to hit due to Dexterity 16, and 10 arrows +1. Has a jeweled pendant worth 1,000 gp. His quarters are adorned with tapestries and carpets — one tapestry alone is worth 500 gp, the finest furnishings in the Central Towers.

## Role

Political and strategic adviser to the Castellan. The third most senior figure in the Keep's chain of command after the Castellan and Captain of the Guard. His presence suggests the Castellan values knowledge and arcane counsel alongside raw military strength.

## Personality & Voice

- Composed and measured; the manner of someone accustomed to advising rather than commanding; he offers assessments, not orders
- Elven patience visible in conversation — he will not be rushed to an opinion; he asks clarifying questions before committing; thinks before speaking
- Intellectually curious; his spells (charm person, web) suggest an interest in controlling situations through cleverness rather than force
- Appreciates quality; his quarters' tapestries and careful furnishings suggest he takes aesthetic satisfaction in his surroundings; this is not vanity, it's taste

## Goals

- Advise Sherlane well; the Castellan's good decisions are the Advisor's primary success metric
- Monitor internal Keep security; he is alert to the possibility that the Outer Bailey visitors include Temple agents
- Contribute to the Caves campaign at the strategic level — where should the Keep focus its limited garrison resources?
- Remain a useful and trusted presence at the Keep long-term; he has no obvious exit agenda

## Knowledge

- Knows the Keep's internal security layout, the garrison's capabilities, and the chain of command
- Can read magic — useful for identifying any documents or items the party brings back from the Caves
- Knows something is off about the Evil Priest Spy's silent acolytes (his assessment, not confirmed); will discuss this if asked directly and in private
- Understands demi-human politics and regional history at a level Sherlane's purely military background does not reach; useful for context questions

## GM Truth

*gm_only*

Despite appearing as a civilian counselor, the Advisor is an extremely capable combatant. His spells — charm person, read magic, web — are crowd-control focused, useful in keep defense. He is more dangerous than any individual in the Outer Bailey.

If the Keep is ever threatened from within (e.g., the spy priest's network escalates), the Advisor will be among the first to respond with force.

**Statistics:** AC 0 (Dex 16, plate mail +1), E3, hp 18, short bow at +2 to hit (Dex), 10 arrows +1, spells: charm person, read magic, web. Ring of fire resistance. ML 12.

**On his person:** 6 pp, 10 gp, jeweled pendant (1,000 gp).

**In his quarters (area 26):** Tapestries and carpets throughout; one tapestry worth 500 gp.
