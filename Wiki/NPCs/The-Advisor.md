# The Advisor

A 3rd-level elf serving as senior counselor to [[Wiki/NPCs/The-Castellan]] at [[Wiki/Locations/Keep-on-the-Borderlands]]. Quartered in the Central Towers (area 26). His high Dexterity and magic-enhanced armor make him the second most defensively capable person in the Keep after the Castellan.

## Description

No physical description given. Wears plate mail +1 and a ring of fire resistance. Carries a short bow used at +2 to hit due to Dexterity 16, and 10 arrows +1. Has a jeweled pendant worth 1,000 gp. His quarters are adorned with tapestries and carpets — one tapestry alone is worth 500 gp, the finest furnishings in the Central Towers.

## Role

Political and strategic adviser to the Castellan. The third most senior figure in the Keep's chain of command after the Castellan and Captain of the Guard. His presence suggests the Castellan values knowledge and arcane counsel alongside raw military strength.

## GM Truth

*gm_only*

Despite appearing as a civilian counselor, the Advisor is an extremely capable combatant. His spells — charm person, read magic, web — are crowd-control focused, useful in keep defense. He is more dangerous than any individual in the Outer Bailey.

If the Keep is ever threatened from within (e.g., the spy priest's network escalates), the Advisor will be among the first to respond with force.

**Statistics:** AC 0 (Dex 16, plate mail +1), E3, hp 18, short bow at +2 to hit (Dex), 10 arrows +1, spells: charm person, read magic, web. Ring of fire resistance. ML 12.

**On his person:** 6 pp, 10 gp, jeweled pendant (1,000 gp).

**In his quarters (area 26):** Tapestries and carpets throughout; one tapestry worth 500 gp.
