# Archclericy of Veluna

Theocratic state in the central Flanaess, governed by the Canon of Veluna (Archcleric), head of the church of Rao. Capital: Mitrik. Veluna is orderly, scholarly, and deeply committed to opposing evil — its clerics and paladins are respected throughout the Flanaess.

## Overview

Devoted to Rao, god of peace, reason, and serenity. The Archcleric holds both secular and religious authority. Veluna maintains close ties with Furyondy and actively opposes the forces of evil from the south and east.

The Archcleric of Veluna co-funds (alongside the Viscount of Verbobonc) the castle construction in [[Wiki/Locations/Village-of-Hommlet]] — reflecting Veluna's ongoing strategic interest in keeping the frontier near the Kron Hills stable and the Temple of Elemental Evil suppressed.

## Relevance to Campaign

The party originated in Veluna. Their mission to investigate humanoid activity and Temple resurgence around Hommlet was authorized by the church in Veluna. The Canon or a bishop could serve as a patron NPC for future mission assignment.

Veluna's church of Rao has agents and interests throughout Verbobonc; their network is distinct from the Viscount's (Elmo et al.) and from the Archcleric's direct agents.
