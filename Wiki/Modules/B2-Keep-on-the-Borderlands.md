# B2 — The Keep on the Borderlands

A classic introductory module featuring a frontier Keep as home base and the nearby Caves of Chaos as the primary dungeon complex. Written by Gary Gygax as a companion to the Basic rulebook.

## Campaign Role

B2 serves as an introductory sandbox. The Keep provides a safe hub with merchants, NPC allies, and hooks; the Caves of Chaos offer a tiered dungeon with seven distinct monster lairs and a shrine to evil chaos. The module introduces faction dynamics, moral complexity (the spy priest), and a hidden conspiracy through the Temple of Evil Chaos.

## Source Material Notes

- The Keep's location is unspecified in the text — it is a generic borderlands frontier. Campaign placement determines its Greyhawk region.
- The spy Priest in area 7b is a sleeper agent of the Temple of Evil Chaos — a significant hidden plot thread.
- The Shrine of Evil Chaos (area K) is the thematic climax of the Caves, housing the evil cleric and undead armies.
- Kobold Lair (A) and Orc Lairs (B, C) have a secret alliance mechanism. Gnolls and Orcs have a loose defensive pact.
- The Hobgoblin Lair (F) holds prisoners including a merchant's wife who rewards rescuers with a dagger +1.
- The Bugbear Clan (H) keeps slaves of multiple humanoid races.
- The Minotaur (I) is a mercenary who works with the bugbears.
- Area 51 (boulder-filled passage in the Shrine) may connect to an exterior exit or a "Cave of the Unknown" — DM's option.
- Monster losses in all lairs cannot be replaced during normal play.
