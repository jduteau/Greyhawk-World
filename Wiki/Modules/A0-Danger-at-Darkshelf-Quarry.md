# A0 — Danger at Darkshelf Quarry

Optional bridge module between the Borderlands arc (T1/B2) and the Slavers arc (A1-4). A short dungeon at a coastal quarry used as a slaver staging post, for characters around level 3–4.

## Campaign Role

Not required, but useful if the party needs an additional level or two before A1, or if they follow the coastal merchant lead from the B2 Shrine amulet breadcrumb. Plants direct evidence of slaver operations that transitions the party into the A-series.
