# G1-3 — Against the Giants

The Giants arc. Military tone, high stakes. [[Wiki/NPCs/Eclavdra]] of House Eilservs has been supplying giant chiefs with enchanted weapons and promises of surface territory. She is physically present in G3 and escapes when the party arrives. This is the first moment drow appear in person.

**DROW TIMELINE:** Eclavdra's appearance in G3 is the first permitted physical drow presence in the campaign. No drow before this point.

## Campaign Role

Layer 6 of [[Wiki/Conspiracy/Lolth-Overarching-Conspiracy]]. The giants are tools who believe they have a patron. The party discovers the patron is drow — and follows that trail into the Underdark.

## Source Material Notes

*Stub — full detail to be added on ingest.*

**Key conspiracy breadcrumbs (G1-3 phase):**
- **G1 (Hill Giant Steading):** Among the chief's treasures, a hand crossbow of drow construction — a gift from their patron. The giant chief calls the patron "the dark lady."
- **G2 (Glacial Rift):** A frost giant jarl has a drow messenger caged as a curiosity. If freed, the messenger will not be grateful — but out of contempt for the giants will tell the party one true thing: *"The giants are tools. Tools get discarded when the work is done."*
- **G3 (Fire Giant Steading):** Eclavdra is present. Aristocratic, coldly beautiful, unconcerned until the party proves capable. Escapes with contemptuous efficiency, collapsing the entrance behind her. A hobgoblin mercenary guard saw her go through a hidden door in the lowest level — he'll tell the party where, for the right price.
