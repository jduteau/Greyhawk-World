# A1-4 — Scourge of the Slavelords

The Slavers arc. Urban intrigue and infiltration, distinct in tone from the frontier/dungeon register of T1/B2. Primary antagonists: [[Wiki/Factions/Brotherhood-of-Slave-Lords]]. The party discovers that the Brotherhood's operations are directed by a drow intermediary — [[Wiki/NPCs/Edralve]] — who the slavers believe is simply a well-connected fence.

## Campaign Role

Layer 4 of [[Wiki/Conspiracy/Lolth-Overarching-Conspiracy]]. The Brotherhood is a tool of the drow. Revealing this connection is the arc's conspiracy payoff.

The transition from B2/T1 to A1-4 is a tonal shift: from frontier sandbox to criminal network infiltration. The party moves into environments where violence is not always the first tool.

**Key conspiracy breadcrumbs (A1-4 phase):**
- Highport (A1): dockmaster's ledger — regular shipments to buyer "E."; destination encodes toward Pomarj coast then down; "the lower road" appears twice
- Edralve's traces: drow-crafted manacles, burned letter fragment referencing "the Great Work above" and "the lords of the deep places"
- Senior Slavelord's hidden chamber (A4): small spider-motif shrine with same cold, heavy quality as the B2 amulet; he received his faith through dreams and has never met a drow
- Captured Slavelord testimony: "a woman — not human, I think — who came twice a year. She wore a hood always. She smelled of deep stone and she never ate. She paid in magic."
