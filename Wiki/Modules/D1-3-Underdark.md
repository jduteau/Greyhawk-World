# D1-3 — Descent into the Depths / Vault of the Drow

The Underdark arc. The party follows Eclavdra's trail into a world they are not prepared for. Survival, navigation, and alien social rules replace military force as the primary challenges. The Vault of the Drow (D3) is a functioning city — the party must navigate it without being captured or killed to reach the portal to the Demonweb.

## Campaign Role

Layer 7 of [[Wiki/Conspiracy/Lolth-Overarching-Conspiracy]]. The full picture becomes visible: [[Wiki/NPCs/Eclavdra]]'s operation was a House Eilservs initiative, not sanctioned by all drow. Some houses are horrified that it has attracted surface-world attention. Lolth herself has said nothing — which means she is pleased.

**Key conspiracy moments (D1-3 phase):**
- **D1-2 (Descent):** The party encounters surface-world slaves who have been underground for years. One may be from a location personal to a party member. Deliberate.
- **D3 (Vault of the Drow):** [[Wiki/NPCs/Eclavdra]] represents House Eilservs — a minority faction. Some drow houses oppose the surface campaign because it has attracted attention.
- **D3 Temple mural:** A spider goddess receiving tribute from giants, slavers, surface cultists, and humanoid warbands — all tiny figures. Not propaganda. Accurate history. The entire campaign, in one image.
- **D3:** Lolth's silence on the House Eilservs operation = approval. The party's presence in the Vault is the moment they become her primary concern.
