# T1 — The Village of Hommlet

A detailed village sourcebook set in the World of Greyhawk, featuring 33 village locations with named NPCs, faction intrigue, and a Temple of Elemental Evil spy network. Used in this campaign for the village and Moat House content only — the T2–T4 continuation is not part of this campaign's module path.

## Campaign Role

T1 provides a rich social sandbox. The village is stable but not safe — Temple of Elemental Evil spies are embedded among the residents, and the ruined Moat House (2–3 miles away, area 33 track) is an active dungeon remnant. The module rewards careful NPC interaction and punishes rushing straight to combat.

Key dramatic tensions:
- Temple spy network is active among apparent merchants and inn patrons
- Village is split between St. Cuthbert faithful and druids — mostly peaceful but distinct
- Burne and Rufus are building a castle and consolidating power with Viscount backing
- The Viscount and Archcleric of Veluna are watching the region for Temple resurgence
- Elmo is a Viscount agent embedded at the inn, available as a party member if paid

## Source Material Notes

- Canonical location: Viscounty of Verbobonc, on the edge of the Kron Hills, near the Gnarley Wood. The Archcleric of Veluna funded the Church of St. Cuthbert here as reward for aid in the war against the Temple.
- The Temple of Elemental Evil was recently defeated but NOT destroyed — agents are already active in Hommlet.
- The ruined Moat House (area 33) is described as "a warning post of the destroyed Temple" and is the primary dungeon for this module.
- Y'dey, the Canoness (6th level cleric), is absent — on a quest with Otis (Elmo's 10th level ranger brother) and Murfles (elf fighter/thief). Her personal cache (bronze neck chain with 6 rubies, 10,000 gp total; +1 mace; scroll of protection from demons) is in the chief priest's chamber fireplace mantle.
- Terjon (6th level cleric) is Y'dey's temporary replacement and is not happy about it.
- Elmo (area 2) knows the village intimately; his "simple drunk ranger" act is cover for the Viscount's intelligence work.
- The traders (Rannos Davi, Gremag) are the most dangerous Temple agents in the village — 10th level thief and 7th level assassin respectively.
- A pass scarab "TZGY" in the traders' quarters allows access to areas of the Temple of Elemental Evil.
