# Temple Spy Thread

The [[Wiki/Factions/Temple-of-Evil-Chaos]] is running an active intelligence and sabotage operation against adventurers based at [[Wiki/Locations/Keep-on-the-Borderlands]]. A planted agent monitors party movements and infiltrates expeditions to the [[Wiki/Locations/Caves-of-Chaos]], then betrays them at a critical moment.

## The Thread

The [[Wiki/NPCs/Shrine-Evil-Cleric]] (Lair K) needs to prevent adventurers from clearing the Caves before they reach the Shrine. His solution: a sleeper agent in the Keep who vets threats before they become dangerous.

[[Wiki/NPCs/Evil-Priest-Spy]] is embedded in the Keep's private apartments (area 7b), posing as a jovial visiting theologian. He has built a flawless social reputation — everyone in the Keep speaks well of him. He is entirely undetected by the [[Wiki/Factions/Keep-Garrison]].

**Intelligence collection:** The spy reads the entry scribe's records in area 3, which log all arrivals and departures. This tells him exactly which parties are heading to the Caves and when. He relays this to the Shrine.

**Active sabotage:** When a promising adventuring party announces plans for the Caves, the spy volunteers to join them. Once inside, during a critical monster encounter, he uses cause light wounds (touch, 2–7 damage) or light (blinds a target) against the party. Betrayal is guaranteed; the only variable is when.

## Known Evidence

Nothing discovered by the party yet. Campaign has not begun.

## Hidden Evidence

*gm_only*

- The spy's hidden sack of coins (30 each of pp/gp/ep/sp/cp + jeweled clasp 300 gp) in his chair at area 7b — a bribe/escape fund inconsistent with his cover story as a traveling theologian
- His two acolytes never speak at all — the "vow of silence" claim is improvised cover; genuine clergy rarely impose blanket silence on junior members
- The Curate's distrust: 50% chance to admit it if asked by a friend
- The Curate's own acolytes enthusiastically defend the spy when asked — this contradiction (senior cleric suspicious, junior clerics effusive) is a detectable pattern
- The spy's spell selection (cause light wounds, light as attack) is offensive-only, inconsistent with a peaceful theological visitor

## Lareth Connection

*gm_only*

The spy [[Wiki/NPCs/Evil-Priest-Spy]] receives monthly written instructions from [[Wiki/NPCs/Lareth-the-Beautiful]] ("the Shepherd") via a goblin courier who travels the road by night. He has been told reinforcements will come from the south if the Caves operation faces serious threat.

**Physical evidence in the Shrine / on the spy priest:**

- A short coded note (merchant symbols) from a Hommlet contact — references supply routes and "the shepherd to the south." Found by searching the priest's body or Shrine chamber.
- Crates in the Shrine stores bearing a trading post mark that matches the Rannos/Gremag traders' establishment in [[Wiki/Locations/Village-of-Hommlet]].

These physical items connect B2 Caves evidence directly to the T1 Hommlet network. The party that finds both the Shrine note and the trading post crates has the thread in their hands. See [[Wiki/Conspiracy/T1-B2-Cross-Module-Thread]] for the full cross-module picture.
