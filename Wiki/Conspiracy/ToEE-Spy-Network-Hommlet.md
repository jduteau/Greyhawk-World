# Temple of Elemental Evil Spy Network — Hommlet

The [[Wiki/Factions/Temple-of-Elemental-Evil]] has embedded a multi-layer intelligence and disruption network throughout [[Wiki/Locations/Village-of-Hommlet]]. All agents report via brigand courier to Temple authority. The party is entirely unaware.

**Distinct from:** [[Wiki/Conspiracy/Temple-Spy-Thread]] (the B2 Caves of Chaos spy network), which involves a different organization.

## The Thread

The Temple was militarily defeated but not destroyed. It has responded by embedding agents in Hommlet to monitor the village, aid Temple servants passing through, track adventuring parties heading to the Moat House, and eliminate successful expeditions before they become a threat to the Temple proper.

The network has three tiers of assets in Hommlet, operating independently — they do not all know each other.

## Network Structure

*gm_only*

**Tier 1 — Primary agents (most dangerous):**
- **[[Wiki/NPCs/Rannos-Davi]]** (area 13, traders) — 10th level thief, CE; runs the traders' establishment; tracks all adventuring activity; reports via brigand courier; has magical reserve for aggressive follow-up
- **Gremag** (area 13, traders) — 7th level assassin, CE; Rannos Davi's partner; dagger of venom + ice dust; "even more chaotic"
- *Rannos and Gremag know about Zert. Zert does not know about them.*

**Tier 2 — Secondary agents:**
- **Zert** (Welcome Wench inn, upper room 1) — 2nd level fighter, CE; poses as merchant awaiting caravan; will join adventuring parties and betray/slay them after a successful run; has medium warhorse + lance in stable; locked coffer with 100 100-gp gems + 100 pp + 265 gp

**Tier 3 — Sleepers and informants:**
- **Groom at traders' barn** (area 13) — 0-level, CE; tracks which parties the traders send their man-at-arms with; reports to traders
- **Spy laborer at castle construction** (area 32) — 2nd level fighter (S 17, CE, +1 dagger); assigned to observe castle construction activity; secretly meets with traders; will report to them and eventually join as a Temple operative when construction is complete

**Communication chain:** Tier 3 → Tier 2 (partial) → Tier 1 (Rannos/Gremag) → brigand courier → Temple hierarchy

## Known Evidence

Nothing discovered by the party yet. Campaign has not begun.

## Hidden Evidence

*gm_only*

Discovery clues (easiest to hardest):
1. **The carter (area 14) dislikes the traders** — can't explain why, but will share his unease if pressed carefully by a customer. This is the lowest-hanging fruit.
2. **Zert at the inn** — his cover story (waiting for a caravan) is thin if pressed; he drinks heavily but never gets drunk (unusual); his locked coffer contains wealth inconsistent with an ordinary traveler
3. **The groom at the traders' barn** — chaotic evil, watching the party; a detect evil or careful observation might flag him
4. **The "TZGY" scarab** in the traders' locked coffer (room 11) — a Temple of Elemental Evil pass; only 20% chance a sage recognizes it; finding it proves the connection
5. **Rannos Davi's medallion vs. crystal balls and ESP** — worn under his jerkin, visible only if searched; indicates awareness of magical surveillance
