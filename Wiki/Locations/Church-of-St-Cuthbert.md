# Church of St. Cuthbert

The Church of St. Cuthbert in [[Wiki/Locations/Village-of-Hommlet]] (area 20). Built by the Viscount of Verbobonc in honor of the Archcleric of Veluna's aid in the war against the Temple of Elemental Evil. A new, well-constructed edifice.

## Overview

The spiritual center of the St. Cuthbert congregation in Hommlet. Newly built. A somewhat distant cleric and assistant officiate — [[Wiki/NPCs/Terjon]] (chief, 6th level, filling in for the absent [[Wiki/NPCs/Y-dey]]) and [[Wiki/NPCs/Calmert]] (assistant, 3rd level). The only source of legitimate holy water in the village; the traders (area 13) sell plain water fraudulently labelled as holy water.

## Geography

**Main Floor:**
- **Room 1 — Hall:** Polished wood floor, wooden pillars, high ceiling. No benches or pews — worshippers stand. Tinted glass mosaic windows in many shades provide colored light.
- **Room 2 — Processional:** Lighter wood floor, deep blue walls with silver-leaf starbursts. Entry for confirmed, married, and ceremonial worshippers led by robed clerics.
- **Room 3 — Altar and Sanctuary:** Screened by deep green velvet drape. Life-size statue of St. Cuthbert (smiling, great cudgel raised, left hand beckoning). Living ferns and plants behind statue. Tree-stump officiant stands. Altar carved from a single piece of bronzewood with billets, starbursts, and the sacred crumpled hat symbol. Walls: carved wainscoting + painted murals of St. Cuthbert's marvels. Holy sayings displayed, used for clerical divination via stick-casting.
- **Room 4 — Study and Audience Chamber:** Where non-holiday visitors must enter first. Hard benches near the door; padded chairs near the fireplace reserved for clergy. Religious tracts and poorbox. Calmert gatekeeps access to Terjon here.
- **Room 5 — Vestry:** Rest and dressing chamber for clergy. Stairs to upper floor.

**Lower Floor (high basement, floored):**
- **Room 6 — Church Kitchen:** Serving woman and husband prepare meals for the clergy.
- **Room 7 — Meditation Room.**
- **Room 8 — Exercise Room:** Club practice conducted here daily (St. Cuthbert's martial tradition).
- **Room 9 — Sacred Trophies Gallery:** Display of dented helmets, maces, clubs, and knobbed cudgels of historic fame.
- **Room 10 — Cells:** For needy laity, acolytes, or confined offenders. Each has only a peg, pallet, and pail. Tiny barred window; heavy iron-bound door bolted from outside.

**Upper Floor:**
- **Room 11 — Church Library:** Religious and legal tomes, librams, scrolls. **Hidden:** Scroll of 7 clerical spells (one per level) + amulet of life protection, concealed inside a thick book titled *"LEGAL AFFAIRS IN VELUNA 2130 312 CY"* — requires active search of the specific volume to find.
- **Room 12 — Upper Hall:** Upholstered chairs and couches for visiting clergy during sermons.
- **Room 13 — Calmert's Chambers:** Quarters for Calmert and any other underpriests. Many bell-ropes with colored beads hang from the center — these operate the collection of bells, chimes, and gongs in the tower above (30' higher). Must be sounded at dawn, noon, dusk, and during Godsday services in specific melodies. One large black bead is actually a lump of platinum (10 gp) — Calmert's entire personal wealth.
- **Room 14 — Chief Priest's Chamber:** Comfortably furnished. **Terjon's:** locked iron coffer under the bed holds church funds (16 pp, 102 gp, 88 ep, 317 sp, 546 cp). **Y'dey's hidden cache:** fireplace mantle lifts to reveal compartments with a bronze neck chain set with 6 × 1,000 gp rubies + amber cudgel pendant (10,000 gp total), +1 mace, and a scroll of protection from demons. Only Y'dey knows this cache exists.
- **Room 15 — Balcony:** ~15' above the main hall floor; the chief cleric delivers weekly sermons from here.

## GM Notes

*gm_only*

- The scroll hidden in the library (room 11) requires the party to specifically search "LEGAL AFFAIRS IN VELUNA" — it won't be found in a general search of the library.
- Y'dey's cache (room 14) is unknown to Terjon. He sleeps in this room but has never opened the fireplace mantle. If the party finds it, they will have to decide whether to take Y'dey's personal property.
- Calmert's bell-rope platinum bead: any character examining the black beads has a chance to notice one is unusually heavy.
- The St. Cuthbert sayings (room 3 altar) can serve as oracle responses to player questions — the GM rolls sticks and reads applicable sayings. Fun in play.
