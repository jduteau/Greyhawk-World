# Veluna

The Archclericy of Veluna is a theocratic state in the Flanaess, devoted to Rao, god of peace, reason, and serenity. Its capital is the holy city of Mitrik. Veluna maintains close ties with Furyondy and actively opposes the forces of evil encroaching from the south and east. The party originated here before travelling to [[Wiki/Locations/Village-of-Hommlet]].

## Overview

Veluna is governed by the Canon of Veluna (Archcleric), head of the church of Rao. The state is orderly, scholarly, and deeply committed to opposing evil. Its clerics and paladins are respected throughout the Flanaess.

The Archcleric of Veluna is noted as a co-sponsor (alongside the Viscount of Verbobonc) of the castle construction at [[Wiki/Locations/Village-of-Hommlet]], reflecting Veluna's ongoing strategic interest in the frontier region near the Kron Hills.

## Geography

- **Capital:** Mitrik (holy city)
- **Borders:** Furyondy (north/east), Bissel (west), Verbobonc (south-east)
- **Terrain:** Fertile lowlands, river valleys

The party — [[Wiki/PCs/Thomas]], [[Wiki/PCs/Aldric]], [[Wiki/PCs/Conrad]], [[Wiki/PCs/Lance]], [[Wiki/PCs/Michael]] — departed Veluna to investigate reports of increased humanoid activity and evil goings-on in the Hommlet region (Session 1).
