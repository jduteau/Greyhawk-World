# Village of Hommlet

A prosperous frontier village in the Viscounty of Verbobonc, situated near the Kron Hills and the Gnarley Wood. Home base for the T1 series. The village is politically stable but honeycombed with [[Wiki/Factions/Temple-of-Elemental-Evil]] spies.

## Overview

Hommlet recovered from the Temple of Elemental Evil war and is thriving. Two religious communities coexist: followers of St. Cuthbert (newer arrivals, associated with the Viscount's backing) and druidical faithful (older, long-established families). The village is governed by an informal council and has a well-organized militia.

The ruined Moat House 2–3 miles away on an overgrown track (area 33) is a remnant warpost of the Temple and the primary dungeon accessible from the village.

## GM Notes

*gm_only*

- The carter (area 14) dislikes the traders (area 13) but can't articulate why — if pressed carefully and as a customer he will share his unease. Good early hook toward the spy conspiracy.
- The potboy at the Welcome Wench (area 7) is an aspiring druid serving as Jaroo's message courier; he reports interesting visitor data to the druid.
- Gundigoot (innkeeper) uses small sliding ceiling panels in the attic to spy on suspicious room occupants via the sliding panels from his loft. He will involve Jaroo if concerned.
- The moneychanger Nira Melubb (area 15) is truly neutral and asks no questions. His personal vault is extremely well defended and stocked.
- Y'dey's private cache (area 20, room 14): only she knows of it. The 10,000 gp necklace + mace +1 + scroll of protection from demons are behind the fireplace mantle.
- The "TZGY" scarab in the traders' quarters (area 13, room 11) is a Temple of Elemental Evil pass — only 20% chance even a sage recognizes it as such.
