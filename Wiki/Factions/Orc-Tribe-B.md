# Orc Tribe B

One of two orc tribes in [[Wiki/Locations/Caves-of-Chaos]], occupying Lair B. Entry is marked by a wall of skulls (human, elven, dwarven) in niches. Maintains a covert alliance with [[Wiki/Factions/Orc-Tribe-C]] via a secret shared meeting room (Room 13). Losses cannot be replaced.

## Overview

Mid-tier cave faction. The watcher at area g. disguises himself as a skull trophy — ducking behind canvas and substituting a goblin head when the party enters. The orc leader is significantly tougher than common orcs (4 HD, +2 damage). A concealed alcove (area i.) holds the lair's best loot including a fireball scroll.

**Approximate strength:** ~8 guards (Rooms 7-8) + 12 males + 18 females + 9 young (non-combatant).

## Leadership

- **Orc Leader B** — Room 12. Chain mail + shield +1 + mace. Fights as 4 HD, hp 15, +2 to damage. 31 gp + ring with gem (700 gp). His room's south tapestry conceals the secret door to [[Wiki/Factions/Orc-Tribe-C]].
- **Two Mates** — fight as male orcs (AC 7, HD 1, hp 3 each); each has 2d6 gp.

## Goals

Territorial control of Lair B. Covert co-operation with Orc Tribe C for mutual defense and planning.

## Party Relationship

Not yet encountered.

## Conspiracy Connections

Secret alliance with [[Wiki/Factions/Orc-Tribe-C]] — the two leaders meet privately in Room 13 (Forgotten Room) to plan cooperative ventures. This room is unknown to anyone else.

## GM Notes

*gm_only*

**Collapse trigger:** If the leader is slain, ALL surviving orcs from Lair B immediately flee to Lair C, taking everything of value. Lair B becomes permanently deserted. This is a significant tactical lever — killing the leader collapses two lairs' worth of coverage if the party can then press Lair C quickly.

**After first attack:** Males from Room 10 will move 4 of their number to Room 9, arm them with crossbows, and lay an ambush for returning adventurers.

**Alcove i (hidden behind Room 12):** 2 chain mail suits (man + dwarf sized), 4 swords, locked iron chest (205 cp + 286 sp + 81 gp + 13 pp). Niche behind a boulder: potion of healing + scroll with a 6-die fireball spell. This is the best single offensive item in the orc areas.

**Room 13 — Forgotten Room:** Only the two orc leaders know of this place. Small table, 2 chairs, a chest (bow, 20 arrows, 2 swords, 2 daggers), 2 shields. Under an old bucket of stagnant water: 2 pouches (each: 1 gem worth 50 gp + 10 gp + 20 sp), guarded by 2 giant centipedes. The centipedes are a passive trap for anyone who blindly grabs the bucket.

## Combat Behavior

*gm_only*

Aggressive and tribal. Orcs B and C are rivals — despite their secret planning room, they will not rush to assist each other in a fight. Don't expect word of combat in Lair B to bring Lair C orcs running. Each tribe treats fighting as its own problem.

On a morale failure, orcs fight desperately rather than scattering — they push harder rather than breaking. Cornered orcs are more dangerous than open-field orcs.
