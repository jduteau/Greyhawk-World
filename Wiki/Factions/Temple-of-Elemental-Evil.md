# Temple of Elemental Evil

A powerful chaotic evil religious organization that was recently (mostly) defeated in a war involving the Viscount of Verbobonc and the Archcleric of Veluna. The Temple is not destroyed — agents are already active in [[Wiki/Locations/Village-of-Hommlet]], and the full Temple complex is detailed in T2.

**Important distinction:** The Temple of Elemental Evil (T1/T2) is a different organization from the [[Wiki/Factions/Temple-of-Evil-Chaos]] (B2). These are separate modules, separate evil factions.

## Overview

The Temple of Elemental Evil operated from a fortified complex in the Viscounty of Verbobonc area. It was militarily defeated, but its organization survived. The ruined Moat House (2–3 miles from Hommlet, accessed via area 33 overgrown track) was one of its warning posts; the Temple proper is further away and detailed in T2.

The Temple has responded to its defeat by embedding a spy network in Hommlet, the nearest significant settlement. This network monitors adventuring activity, aids Temple servants passing through, and will respond aggressively if the party begins disrupting Temple operations.

## Leadership

- **Shrine master (T1 level):** Unknown to party. The Hommlet spy network reports via brigand courier to higher Temple authority.
- **Hommlet agents:** [[Wiki/NPCs/Rannos-Davi]] (+ Gremag, his assassin partner); Zert (fighter at Welcome Wench inn); chaotic evil groom at traders; spy laborer at castle construction (area 32)

## Goals

**True goals (gm_only):** Monitor and suppress adventuring activity that threatens Temple operations. Aid Temple servants passing through Hommlet. Gather intelligence on the village's military capability (especially Burne and Rufus). Eventually restore Temple power in the region.

**Immediate goals:** Track all adventurers heading to the Moat House. Ensure no Temple secrets are discovered and reported. Eliminate successful adventuring parties before they grow strong enough to threaten the Temple proper.

## Party Relationship

Unknown to party.

## GM Notes

*gm_only*

The "TZGY" scarab in the traders' quarters (area 13, room 11 locked coffer) is an access pass to an area of the Temple of Elemental Evil — significant foreshadowing for T2. Only a 20% chance even a sage identifies it.

The Moat House (accessed from area 33 overgrown track) is the primary dungeon for T1 and connects to the Temple's larger operations. It was explicitly "a warning post of the destroyed Temple."

Once the party begins attacking Temple interests (raiding the Moat House, exposing traders, neutralizing spies), the Temple will escalate. The traders' magical reserve is available for precisely this purpose.
