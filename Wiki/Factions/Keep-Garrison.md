# Keep Garrison

The military and administrative force that maintains and defends [[Wiki/Locations/Keep-on-the-Borderlands]]. A lawful-aligned frontier garrison under the command of [[Wiki/NPCs/The-Castellan]].

## Overview

A well-organized frontier garrison operating in layered defense: outer bailey watch, inner gatehouse guards, and cavalry reserves in the Keep Fortress itself. Strict entry protocols — all visitors disarmed and escorted; a scribe records all entry/exit. The garrison is an ally to lawful adventurers, not a threat.

**Approximate total strength:**
- Outer: corporal of the watch (F2), Bailiff (F3), captain of the watch (F3) with ~60 men-at-arms in towers 2, 6, 12
- Inner: Captain of the Guard (F3), Sergeant of Guard (F2, Str 17), ~80 guardsmen across gatehouse/towers; 30 cavalrymen in Keep Fortress
- Senior staff: Castellan (F6), Scribe (C2), Advisor (E3)

## Leadership

- [[Wiki/NPCs/The-Castellan]] — Supreme commander, area 27; absolutely brave and honest
- [[Wiki/NPCs/Captain-of-the-Guard]] — Inner gatehouse (F3, AC 0, sword+2, spear+1, hp 24); kind, friendly, excellent leader; sometimes disguises as a mercenary in the Outer Bailey
- **Bailiff** — Outer bailey superintendent (F3, magic plate+1 or sword+1, hp 22)
- **Captain of the Watch** — Watch tower commander (F3, sword+2, dagger+1, hp 20)
- **Corporal of the Watch** — Entry yard (F2, sword+1, hp 15, grouchy but respects brave fighters; susceptible to a pretty girl)
- **Sergeant of the Guard** — Inner gatehouse (F2, Str 17, sword+1, dagger+1, potion of healing; loves drinking and brawling)
- [[Wiki/NPCs/The-Advisor]] — Senior elf counselor, area 26 (E3, Dex 16, plate+1, charm/web spells)
- [[Wiki/NPCs/The-Curate]] — Spiritual authority, not technically part of military chain but highly influential

## Goals

Defend the borderlands, maintain order in the Keep, support lawful adventurers who seek to clear the [[Wiki/Locations/Caves-of-Chaos]] and reduce the humanoid threat.

## Party Relationship

Not yet encountered.

## Conspiracy Connections

The garrison is entirely unaware that [[Wiki/NPCs/Evil-Priest-Spy]] (area 7b) is a spy for [[Wiki/Factions/Temple-of-Evil-Chaos]]. The Curate's private distrust of the priest is the only active suspicion within the Keep's leadership.

## GM Notes

*gm_only*

The garrison is a resource, not an obstacle — assuming the party behaves lawfully. Key pressure points and opportunities:

- **Castellan's hasty decisions:** He can be moved to act quickly on a convincing urgent framing. Useful if the party needs an official response to something.
- **Guild Master influence (area 16):** Very influential with fortress personnel. His favor or dislike affects how garrison members treat the party. Getting on his good side is worth pursuing early.
- **Entry scribe (area 3):** Records all comings and goings. The spy priest monitors these records — the party should be aware their movements are known to the Shrine.
- **Captain of the Guard's disguise:** Occasionally wanders the Outer Bailey as a mercenary. He could be a useful hidden contact if the party treats mercenaries well.
- **Senior staff combat power:** The Advisor (E3, area 26) and Scribe (C2, area 26) are much more dangerous than they look — relevant if the Keep itself is ever threatened or the party causes trouble.
