# Brotherhood of the Slave Lords

A sophisticated criminal organization running slave operations across the coastal region. Primary antagonists of the A-series (levels 4–8). Senior leadership receives direction from a drow intermediary they believe to be a fence.

## Overview

The Brotherhood operates slaving raids, trafficking operations, and a network of staging posts between the Pomarj coast and inland markets. They are competent and well-organized — not a raider band but a functioning criminal enterprise with hierarchy, compartmentalization, and resources.

Their key vulnerability: the senior Slavelords do not know who they are truly working for. [[Wiki/NPCs/Edralve]] has been directing their operations toward ends that serve Lolth's conspiracy. The Brotherhood believes they have a powerful, well-connected patron. They are tools.

## Leadership

Senior Slavelords — specific identities in A1-4 module text. One is a secret Lolth worshipper who received his faith through dreams; he has never met a drow. His hidden chamber (A4) contains a small spider-motif shrine with the same cold, heavy quality as the B2 Shrine amulet.

## Goals

**Stated goals:** Profit from slaving operations; expand coastal territory; accumulate wealth and influence.

**True purpose (gm_only):** Serve as a logistics and acquisition network for Lolth's destabilization campaign — channeling slaves, intelligence, and resources toward the Underdark via "the lower road."

## GM Notes

*gm_only*

The A-series is urban intrigue and infiltration, distinct from the dungeon-crawl tone of T1/B2. The party moves from a frontier/local-mystery register into a criminal network register.

The slaver operation connects backward to the coastal merchant breadcrumb from the B2 Shrine amulet — if the party followed that thread, it leads here. If they didn't, Highport (A1) is the on-ramp.
