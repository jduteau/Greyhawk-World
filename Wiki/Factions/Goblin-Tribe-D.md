# Goblin Tribe D

The most numerous and active humanoid faction in [[Wiki/Locations/Caves-of-Chaos]], occupying Lair D. Wandering patrols make the lair unusually dynamic — the party has a 1-in-6 chance of an encounter for every 10' traveled. When encountered, goblins attack and shout "Bree-Yark!" (an alarm cry). Losses cannot be replaced.

## Overview

Large tribe constantly moving supplies and messages through their tunnels. Exploited from above by the [[Wiki/Factions/Hobgoblin-Tribe-F]], who steal their best food through a secret door into Room 21. The goblins are unaware of this theft. In desperation, Room 18 guards will toss 250 gp to the Ogre (Lair E) through a secret door to buy his mercenary service against intruders.

**Approximate strength:** 12 guards (Rooms 17-18) + 10 males + 14 females + 6 young (Room 19) + chieftain + 3 guards + females (Room 20). Plus up to 6 wandering males in the tunnels.

## Leadership

- **Goblin Chieftain** — Room 20. AC 4 (chain mail + shield), HD 3, hp 11, D 2-7 (Strength + skill). 18 gp + 2 pp in purse. Silver cup (90 gp) under the bed. Bow on wall (will use if time allows). 3 elite guards (AC 6, HD 1+1, hp 7) flank him.
- **Hidden treasure:** Secret drawer under the bench seat holds a tapestry woven with silver and gold thread worth **900 gp** — the most valuable single item in the goblin area, and easily missed without searching the furniture.

## Goals

Survival, supply-hoarding, and keeping the Ogre on retainer as emergency muscle.

## Party Relationship

Not yet encountered.

## Conspiracy Connections

Emergency mercenary relationship with the Ogre in Lair E (250 gp bribe hidden in water barrel in Room 18, adjacent to the secret door). No connection to [[Wiki/Factions/Temple-of-Evil-Chaos]]. Exploited by [[Wiki/Factions/Hobgoblin-Tribe-F]] who steal their food without their knowledge.

## GM Notes

*gm_only*

**Wandering encounter rule:** Check every 30' (3-in-6 cumulative chance). Wandering group of 6 males attacks and shouts "Bree-Yark!" simultaneously — they don't flee first. Also carry d6 bags of good foodstuffs (suitable for human consumption, minor resupply value).

**Ogre bribe (Room 18):** Sack of 250 gp hidden in a water barrel next to the secret door. If Room 18 guards hear "Bree-Yark!", 2 will toss the sack through the secret door to the Ogre and ask for help. Ogre arrives immediately. If the party finds and takes the sack before this happens, the goblins lose their panic button.

**Hobgoblin theft:** Hobgoblins enter through a secret door in Room 21 to steal the best supplies. If the party stays in Room 21 for more than 1 turn, 4 hobgoblins come through that door.

**Defeat behavior:** If soundly losing, goblins attempt to hide or flee east from Room 17 to Room 23 (Hobgoblin Common Room) and join forces. Adjust hobgoblin numbers accordingly.

**900 gp tapestry:** In the secret drawer under the chieftain's bench. Only found if the party specifically searches the furniture.

## Combat Behavior

*gm_only*

Cowardly and disorganised. Rely on numbers and ambush rather than direct confrontation. Break morale quickly — goblins scatter rather than die. Cruel to the weak, craven before the strong.

On a morale failure, individuals scatter in different directions rather than retreating as a unit. Do not expect a rallied last stand. The "Bree-Yark!" shouting is alarm and aggression simultaneously — they are calling for help while attacking, not organizing a defense.
