# Kobold Tribe A

The weakest humanoid faction in [[Wiki/Locations/Caves-of-Chaos]], occupying Lair A. Notable for a covered pit trap 30' inside the entrance and a dangerous rat colony kept as pets. Losses cannot be replaced.

## Overview

Small tribe with an elaborate layered defense: outside ambush, pit trap, and guard rooms feeding into a common chamber. The kobold chieftain is surprisingly tough (HD 2, battle axe) and wears a large gem on a golden chain worth 1,200 gp. The tribe keeps 18 giant rats as pets in Room 2 — they rush to any noise or combat.

**Approximate strength:** ~6 guards + 17 males + 23 females + 8 young (non-combatant). 3 elite guards in chain mail (Room 4).

## Leadership

- **Kobold Chieftain** — Room 5. AC 5, HD 2, hp 8, battle axe (D 2-8). Key to the food storage (Room 3). Large gem on golden chain (1,200 gp). Hidden 50 gp sewn into hem of a wall-hanging blanket. Locked chest: 203 cp + 61 sp + 22 ep.

## Goals

Survival and defense of the lair. Keep the giant rat colony fed. Avoid the orcs and other cave factions.

## Party Relationship

Not yet encountered.

## Conspiracy Connections

No connection to [[Wiki/Factions/Temple-of-Evil-Chaos]] or any other cave faction. The most isolated tribe in the Caves.

## GM Notes

*gm_only*

**Entrance hazards:**
- 2-in-6 chance of 8 kobolds ambushing from trees before the party even enters.
- Pit trap 30' inside: 3-in-6 front rank falls (10' deep, 1-6 damage, lid closes, trapped until freed from outside). Noise attracts Rooms 1 and 2.
- Planks to cross the pit are stored inside Room 1.

**Tactical notes:**
- If the party hurls oil, the kobolds retreat rather than stand and burn.
- One guard from Room 1 will run to warn Rooms 4 and 6 on any incursion.
- Giant rats (Room 2): 18 rats rush to battle noise. Each bite has 1-in-20 chance of disease (save vs. Poison or die in 1-6 days / too sick to adventure for 1 month).
- Rat leader wears thin silver chain + 5 small gems (400 gp total) — easily missed if the party doesn't specifically search the rat pack.

**Silk piece** worth 150 gp is hidden in the litter of Room 6 — not found unless the party searches.
