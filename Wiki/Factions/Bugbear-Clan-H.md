# Bugbear Clan H

A small but physically powerful faction in [[Wiki/Locations/Caves-of-Chaos]], occupying Lair H. Fewer in number than the goblin or orc tribes but superior individual combatants. Known for elaborate deception — welcoming signs in multiple languages at the entrance, guards who offer food before attacking. Keep slaves in two pens (Rooms 40-41). Losses cannot be replaced.

## Overview

The bugbear clan maintains slave pens populated with a diverse mix of humanoid races and at least two significant human-level threats (a rebel bugbear and a berserk evil hero). The chieftain has an escape route directly to [[Wiki/NPCs/The-Minotaur]]'s lair and knows how to activate the mercenary relationship. Two bugbears are always out hunting when the party first arrives.

**Approximate strength:** 3 guards (Room 35) + chieftain + female mate (Room 36) + 3 males + 7 females + 3 young (Room 38) + 2 guards + 3 females (Room 39). Plus 2 hunters who return the day after first party entry.

## Leadership

- **Bugbear Chieftain** — Room 36. AC 5, HD 4+1, hp 18, D 3-12 (d10+2). Hand axe +1 on wall (hurls first, then closes to melee). Key to Room 37 in his pouch (with 29 pp + 3 gems at 50 gp each). Knows the secret door to Lair I (his escape route to the Minotaur). Gray chest on ceiling ledge: 1,462 sp + alabaster/ivory statue (200 gp) + 2 potions of healing (break if chest is roughly handled).
- **Bugbear Chieftain's Mate** — Room 36. AC 5, HD 3+1, hp 12, D 2-8. Gold earrings (100 gp). Fights as a full combatant.

## Goals

Prey acquisition and slave-keeping. The hunting parties suggest an active raiding posture. The chieftain maintains the Minotaur as a fallback ally, which implies strategic awareness unusual for a cave faction.

## Party Relationship

Not yet encountered.

## Conspiracy Connections

Mercenary relationship with [[Wiki/NPCs/The-Minotaur]] (1 human slave per 3 days of service). The chieftain will call on the Minotaur only when his own life is in serious danger. No connection to [[Wiki/Factions/Temple-of-Evil-Chaos]].

## GM Notes

*gm_only*

**Deceptive welcome:** Signs outside the entrance (in kobold, orcish, goblin, etc.) offer "safety, security and repose." Guards in Room 35 offer skewers of toasting meat, then use them as weapons with +2 to hit for surprise.

**Prisoner hooks (Rooms 40-41):**

Room 40: 3 kobolds, 1 goblin, 4 orcs, 2 humans (+ optional 1 dwarf, 2 elves). All fight bugbears if armed. Humans behave as hobgoblin prisoners (serve rescuers 1 year for room/board + gear). Dwarf/elves help while party remains in the Caves.

Room 41 contains the two dangerous prisoners:
- **Rebel bugbear** (hp 14): Hates his fellow bugbears. Will fight all other Caves inhabitants as long as the party remains there. Reliable combat ally.
- **The Hero** (F4, Str 18, hp 24, evil): Berserk fury in combat — 50% per round chance of hitting a friendly target. Once calm: will attempt to kill or rob rescuers if he thinks he can win; otherwise steals the most valuable item and slips away. Do not let the party assume he is grateful.

Keys to Rooms 40 and 41 hang on the wall opposite the stairs in Room 39.

**Hunters return:** 2 bugbears out hunting come back the day after the party's first entry, bringing a human corpse and 83 gp. They can be placed as Room 35 reinforcements.

**Chieftain escalation:** Chieftain flees to the Minotaur only when his own life is in genuine peril. Once he activates that alliance, the Minotaur enters the Caves and hunts the party.

**Room 37 (Spoils Room):** Locked with chieftain's key. Shield +1 (used as an herb tray), quality foodstuffs + leather hides + 3 barrels ale + tun wine + small keg oil. Total sale value at the Keep: 400 gp (excluding shield and oil).
