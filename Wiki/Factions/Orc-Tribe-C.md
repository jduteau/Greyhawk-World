# Orc Tribe C

The second orc tribe in [[Wiki/Locations/Caves-of-Chaos]], occupying Lair C. More passive in its watch posture than [[Wiki/Factions/Orc-Tribe-B]] — relies on a trip-wire net alarm rather than active guards. The leader is the most mobile combatant among the orc chiefs, capable of hurling a hand axe +1 and still attacking normally in the same round. Losses cannot be replaced.

## Overview

Lair C uses a nearly invisible camouflaged net (10'×18', tarred rope) strung across the entry passage. When tripped, the net drops and metal pieces create an alarm. Guards from Room 14 arrive in 1 round. The net can be spotted on 1-in-6 per careful observer. The leader's quarters contain a rope of climbing tied around a sack of 50 platinum pieces — one of the highest coin-value items in the Caves.

**Approximate strength:** 9 males + 8 females + 3 young (Room 14); 6 males + 4 females (Room 15).

## Leadership

- **Orc Leader C** — East area. Plate mail + shield + sword. AC 2, HD 3, hp 16, Save F3, ML 10. Hand axe +1 at belt: can hurl it AND attack normally in the same round. Silver belt + gold buckle (160 gp total); sword with 100 gp gem in pommel; 8 gp + 17 ep + 5 sp. Small chest of drawers holds sack of 50 pp tied with a **rope of climbing**.
- **Mate** — Fights as a male orc (AC 7, HD 1, hp 5, ML 10). Ivory bracelet worth 100 gp.

## Goals

Territorial defense. Maintain covert alliance with Orc Tribe B. Accumulate wealth (the 50 pp sack suggests successful raiding).

## Party Relationship

Not yet encountered.

## Conspiracy Connections

Secret alliance with [[Wiki/Factions/Orc-Tribe-B]] via the shared Forgotten Room (Room 13). The two leaders meet there to plan joint ventures and discuss tribal problems. If Orc B's leader is slain and B's survivors flee to C, this tribe absorbs them.

## GM Notes

*gm_only*

**Net alarm:** Spotted on 1-in-6 per careful observer. Once tripped, Room 14 orcs arrive in 1 round, Room 15 in 3 rounds. The camouflaged net traps victims for 1-4 rounds.

**After attack:** If this tribe is hit, Room 15 males watch the entrance, ready to ambush a second incursion.

**Collapse trigger:** If the leader is slain, survivors flee to Lair B if it still stands; otherwise, they abandon the Caves entirely.

**Copper bowl misdirection (leader's quarters):** A finely wrought copper bowl chased with silver sits filled with garbage, very tarnished — looks worth 10 sp, actually worth 50 gp. Easy to discard without close inspection.

**Room 13 — Forgotten Room:** Shared with [[Wiki/Factions/Orc-Tribe-B]] leader. Under an old bucket of stagnant water: 2 pouches (each: 1 gem worth 50 gp + 10 gp + 20 sp), guarded by 2 giant centipedes.

## Combat Behavior

*gm_only*

Aggressive and tribal. Rivals with Orc Tribe B — they will not coordinate willingly during a fight even if they can hear it nearby. The secret alliance is for planning and mutual benefit, not battlefield mutual aid.

Like Tribe B, orcs C fight desperately under pressure rather than scattering. If the leader is killed, survivors don't scatter — they evacuate toward Lair B, which makes rapid sequential attacks on both lairs possible if the party can exploit the collapse window.
