# Session 1 — Hommlet and the Keep

## Summary

The party arrived in Hommlet from Veluna and gathered intelligence from key NPCs — [[Wiki/NPCs/Gundigoot]] the innkeeper, [[Wiki/NPCs/Terjon]] at the Church of St. Cuthbert, [[Wiki/NPCs/Burne]] and [[Wiki/NPCs/Rufus]] at the tower, and the druid [[Wiki/NPCs/Jaroo-Ashstaff]]. They learned of suspicious activity at the old [[Wiki/Locations/Moat-House]] and increased humanoid coordination in the region. Following the advice of multiple sources, they traveled to the [[Wiki/Locations/Keep-on-the-Borderlands]] and met with [[Wiki/NPCs/Sherlane]], who briefed them on the [[Wiki/Locations/Caves-of-Chaos]] — the apparent source of coordinated humanoid raids — and offered payment for actionable intelligence. The party now prepares to investigate the Caves.

## Narrative

The company of five — [[Wiki/PCs/Thomas]] the paladin, his brother [[Wiki/PCs/Michael]] the mage, [[Wiki/PCs/Aldric]] the cleric, [[Wiki/PCs/Lance]] the ranger, and [[Wiki/PCs/Conrad]] the thief — arrived in the [[Wiki/Locations/Village-of-Hommlet]] after three days travel from [[Wiki/Locations/Veluna]]. The church had sent them to investigate troubling rumors of increased humanoid activity and possible evil stirrings in the region, fears heightened by the memory of the [[Wiki/Factions/Temple-of-Elemental-Evil]] that once held sway over these lands.

Their first stop was the [[Wiki/Locations/Welcome-Wench-Inn]], where the jovial innkeeper [[Wiki/NPCs/Gundigoot]] provided rooms and meals. There they encountered two figures of interest: [[Wiki/NPCs/Elmo]], a large and seemingly simple local who offered his services as a guide, and [[Wiki/NPCs/Zert]], a sharp-eyed stranger claiming to await a caravan who took particular interest in the party's intentions and offered to join them.

Thomas and Aldric visited [[Wiki/NPCs/Terjon]] at the [[Wiki/Locations/Church-of-St-Cuthbert]], finding a stern but practical man troubled by reports of activity near the old Moat House and deeply concerned about the missing [[Wiki/NPCs/Y-dey]], who had left to investigate the Temple situation and never returned. Terjon counseled them to seek out Sherlane at the Keep on the Borderlands before taking action.

Meanwhile, Conrad engaged Zert in conversation, learning that the stranger knew of the Moat House and its dark history, though Conrad sensed the man was gathering as much information as he gave.

The following morning, the party met with [[Wiki/NPCs/Burne]] and [[Wiki/NPCs/Rufus]] at their tower. The magic-user Burne proved calculating and thorough, questioning their capabilities and warning them that while the Moat House needed investigation, they should be cautious of Zert — a stranger taking interest in who investigates the ruins was suspicious. He recommended taking Elmo if they needed a local guide, and provided a map of the area.

A visit to the druid Jaroo Ashstaff in his grove south of town confirmed the pattern: something was wrong at the Moat House, but Jaroo too counseled seeking Sherlane's intelligence before acting. He asked them to bring back any symbols or markings they found.

Heeding the consensus, the party struck out northwest for the Keep on the Borderlands. They camped overnight without incident, though Michael spotted seven figures traveling the same road before dawn. At the Keep, they were granted audience with Castellan Sherlane, who confirmed the dire situation: humanoid tribes in the Caves of Chaos were acting with unnatural coordination, and travelers had been disappearing — taken, not killed. Sherlane offered the Keep as a base of operations and promised payment for intelligence, particularly anything revealing who or what directed the humanoid forces.

The party now stands in the Keep's inner bailey, preparing to venture into the Caves of Chaos.
